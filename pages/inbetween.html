Over the Cee Scenario #1

Mission Briefing:

Slight overview:

It's the 18th of November 1918 and Latvia has declared itself a new republic! Currently in it's territory resides the German VI. Reserve Corps.

Do not worry, it is tasked to defend Latvia from larger threats. Although the Red Army has invaded Latvia and taken over most of it's territory in 2 months.

Now it is up to you Commander, to combine forces with the German VI. Reserve Corps and the Latvian National Army to take back our rightful territory!

High Command has issued new orders for the upcoming operation:

  • Take back the cities of Rīga and Cēsis!
  • Do not rely on the Germans too much, have atleast 1 Latvian Division alive by the end.
  • You cannot lose the city of Liepāja, even if the Soviets do not annex Liepāja, you cannot lose it, this will result in an immediate loss.
  • Do not push into Latgale, we do not have the resources to sustain our army so far. (This region has been marked in a darker colour)
